We understand that our students are busy, and we want to help. Whether you are going on holiday or just feel like taking a day off, you are able to manage by canceling your upcoming classes and make-up lessons day extension.

2. How many students are in the class?

Our classes are designed as individually because we understand the importance of students' requirements that’s why we have one-to-one class.

3. Is there an intensive course for me? (everyday, etc.)

Yes! Our Essential English classes run 5 days a week, so you can practice day-to-day with a trainer.

4. Do I get my choice of a trainer?

We always try to mix-match trainers from all over India as per your comfortableness. Yes, you can have a choice of trainer.

You Need the Patience to Become Fluent in a Language, True language fluency requires consistent effort and time.
